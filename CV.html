Research Interests                                                                                            

 

My interests broadly include disease ecology, epidemiological modeling, statistics,
pollinator conservation and computer science. Advised by Dr. Allison Brody and Dr. Nicholas Gotelli,
my graduate work broadly aims to examine how RNA viruses and the microsporidian parasite (Nosema spp.)
spread from one species of bee to another and how these two pathogens interact within the host.
Using a combination of mathematical modeling and empirical work, I hope to better understand how disease
spillover, temporal variation in disease load and prevalence influence patterns of co-infection in
both native and managed bees.
