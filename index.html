P. Alexander Burnham

I am a 3rd year Ph.D. candidate in the department of biology at the University of Vermont. In addition, I am student at the Vermont Complex Systems Center, assistant coordinator for the National Honeybee Survey in Vermont and founder of the Computational Bee Lab.

About the CBL

Our goal is to bring biologists and computer scientists together to think about pollinator health in a new way. The CBL's mission is to use novel and innovative computational tools in conjunction with experimental biology to increase our understanding of pollinator declines and find new ways to reduce them.
